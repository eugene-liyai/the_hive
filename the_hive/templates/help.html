Side Navigation panel

The side navigation gives you access to the various pages accessible in the application. Through this navigation panel, you are able to access the profile page, set your availability and just to state the obvious, you are also able to log out.

Profile panel

The profile panel allows you access to your profile information. This information is useful when conducting your `hive` day to day activities.

Profile Action

The Action button in the profile panel allows you to edit your profile, change your password, view account stats and an extra link that enables you set your availability.

Availability panel

As the name suggests, this panel marks your availability to receive jobs/tasks. When set to 'Yes', your account will be available for assignment of jobs.

Jobs Panel

The jobs panel list all jobs that your account has been assigned. You can get more information on a job by clicking its row in the table. You can also click the table heading cells to sort the row in ascending or descending order.
